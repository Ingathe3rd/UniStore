Our Mission

UniStore was born from a simple observation: university students have unique needs that aren't met by mainstream marketplaces. We created a platform specifically designed for campus life - connecting students who want to buy, sell, and trade books, food, services, and more.

Our mission is to foster a sustainable, supportive university community where students can save money, reduce waste, and build connections through peer-to-peer commerce. We believe in the power of local exchange to make student life more affordable and sustainable.

How to Post Your Item

Ready to sell your items on UniStore? It's simple!

To get your product or service listed, please send us the following details via WhatsApp:

  • Title of your post
  • Price
  • Description of the item or service
  • Your Seller Name
  • Your Contact Details (e.g., WhatsApp number, email)

Once we receive your information, our team will take care of getting your post live on the site. You can contact any of the team members.

Posting Costs & Duration

Each post costs R20 and will remain active on the site for one month. If you wish for your post to remain visible after this period, another payment will be required to relist it.

Boost Your Post!

During the month your post is active, you can choose to boost it to the top of the page for a small fee of R10. This helps your item get more visibility!
